Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

iCT Dose does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. This app operates entirely offline on your device.

Information We Do Not Collect

iCT Dose is a local reference tool. We do not collect:

  • Personal identification information
  • Patient data of any kind
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or diagnostics
  • Device identifiers
  • Health or medical information

How the App Works

All reference data, dose values, and calculations performed within iCT Dose are processed entirely on your device. No data is sent to external servers. No internet connection is required or used during normal app operation.

BMI Calculator

Height and weight values entered into the BMI Guide feature are used only for on-device calculation. These values are not stored between sessions and are never transmitted off your device.

Third-Party Services

iCT Dose does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Apple App Store

iCT Dose is distributed through the Apple App Store. Apple may collect certain usage and diagnostic data as part of their standard platform operations. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for details.

Children's Privacy

iCT Dose is intended for use by qualified medical professionals and students. The app is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
